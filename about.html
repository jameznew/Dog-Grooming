About All Scruff An' Crufts...

Hi my name is Debbie. After working 34years in manufacturing, the last 18 years at one of the leading automotive companies I decided to train as a professional dog groomer. I offer a home salon service situated in the Wirral. I have been trained to the City & Guilds module . I travelled to Norwich where I trained at Daper Dogs. Not only was the dog grooming training excellent but I was also taught to work with the dogs in a calm and collective manner. Your dog will get plenty of cuddles and praise with me.

My salon is in the basement of my house. No expense has been spared in getting a clean safe environment for your dog. my equipment is all new and to the highest standard. Also my products are organic and eco friendly.

I understand dog owners busy schedules. I understand you would like your dog groomed the day you phone. I will do my upmost to fit all calls in within 48 hours. Also I work weekends and I can keep hold of your dog until you are ready to pick them up.

I also do early starts and late finishes. I offer these services because as a dog owner I know how frustrating it is trying to get an appointment to suite.
